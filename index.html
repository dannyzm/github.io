STEP@UNC

STEP: Striving Toward Educational Prosperity. Founded in 2009, we bring around 1,000 North Carolinian elementary and middle school students to UNC’s campus each spring, with the aim of exposing youth to higher education and sharing how a Bachelor’s degree can be made possible and affordable.
